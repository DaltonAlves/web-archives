Corcoran Gallery of Art - Web Archives of the Final Days

In October 2014, the oldest art museum in Washington D.C., the Corcoran Gallery of Art closed its doors. This digital exhibition provides insight into the final days of the Corcoran by allowing users to explore archived versions of websites from 2014.
