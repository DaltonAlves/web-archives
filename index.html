Corcoran Gallery of Art: A Web Archive of Its Closing

In October 2014, the oldest art museum in Washington D.C., the Corcoran Gallery of Art closed its doors. This digital exhibition provides insight into the final days of the Corcoran by allowing users to explore archived versions of websites from 2014.

Save the Corcoran

Please note, external links in the above archived version of the website will not work. They were not archived in 2014 when this page was captured by archivists.

In June 2012, students, faculty, staff, and friends of the Corcoran Gallery came together to form "Save the Corcoran" in an effort to prevent the Gallery from closing. This initiative was sparked when it was revealed that the Gallery's Trustees were seeking to appraise the value of the Museum building, suggesting potential plans to sell the property. By June 2014, the group was granted the opportunity to present its case for preserving the Corcoran during the court proceedings required for the official closure of the Gallery.
